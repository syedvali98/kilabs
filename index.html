About Us

KI-LABS We desire to supplement the learning of students from just theoretical into complete and overall learning by offering student centric practical cum live experimental classes right at your door step. We would like to extend this good opportunity to offer state of the art, class wise and month wise practical experiments under highly trained and experienced tutors. Our primary aim is to harness the talent of the children rather just demonstration. We are looking forward to joining hands with you in harnessing the budding talents in your Students.

Services

What We Do.

We at KI-LABS introduce a full-fledged Science Laboratory in your School for the students of Class V to X, KI-LABS are strictly as per the recommendations of the SCERT and also Continuous Comprehensive Evaluation (CCE) methods.

Register

Once the school is registered with KI-LABS, we arrange a visit of our trainers and mentors who would interact with the Principal and the Science Teachers and draw a road map. Accordingly entire planning is tailored as per the requirements and demand of the institution.

Plan

The admin team of KI-LABS will co-ordinate with the School Management or the in- charge of KI-LABS Program at School to schedule the dates and no of experiments.

Execute

Exclusive hand out material having details of experiments, list of essentials, procedure, observation and results will be supplied to the school in advance so as to help the students get prepared.

Train

On the scheduled day, KI-LABSmobile team will visit the School comprising of a Lead Instructor, Trainer and Volunteers to start off the KI-LABS Program at School.

Benefits

• The School will have a full-fledged Science mobile Laboratory,
• School need not have to allot any special room for Science Lab,
• Any existing classroom with proper ventilation can be converted into a KI-LABS,
• No need of having any special need of Science Laboratory Staff,
• No need of spending heavy costs on Science Equipment and its maintenance,
• KI-LABS expert team of Trainers, Mentors & Volunteers will take care of Science Practical’s under a strict protocol.
• All KI-LAB Straining and procedures are documented and photo/video recorded,
• Every student will get opportunity to practice & perform laboratory experiments,
• Suitable opportunity to encourage scientific temper in students and also harness their inert talents,
• KI-LABS process photos/videos will be shared with School Managements,
• Exclusive opportunity for Schools to advertise regarding practical training, etc.
• Students will get a chance to participate in inter school science Olympiads, etc.
• Very low cost of subscription per child,
• Every School will get Study Material, Question Papers, Intra School and Inter School Competition participation.
